1. Open Gimp

2. MediaScripting -> "DBus Server"

3. Open emacs

4. File -> Visit New File [c-x c-f]

5. Click on the lowest most white space

6. A sub-window should appear, click within it to select it

7. Hold down your alt key and press x simultaneously

8. This will bring up a prompt in the bottom whitespace

9. Type shell in here

10. Select the lower sub-window, and type the three letters irb,
then press enter.

-You can resize the bottom window by clicking and dragging the middle
barrier
